Sushant Agarwal

Hello, I recently graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, where I was advised by Shai Ben-David. Previously, I did my Bachelor's in Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute. I am broadly interested in Theoretical Machine Learning, and ethical issues (such as Fairness, Interpretability, Privacy, and Robustness) in ML algorithms. I am motivated by problems that are theoretically challenging, while also being practically relevant.
